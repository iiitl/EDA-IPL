## E4: Venue Chasing vs. Defending Paradise (Match-level)

Some stadiums have short boundaries; others have evening dew that makes the ball hard to grip for bowlers in the second innings. Which venues heavily favor the team batting second?

### The Objective
Determine the statistical advantage of batting first vs. second for different stadiums.

### Requirements:
1. Identify the top 10 venues by total matches hosted using `matches.csv`.
2. For these venues, calculate the ratio of matches won by the team batting first vs. the team batting second (chasing).
3. **Visualization:** Generate a **horizontal grouped bar plot** (or a heatmap) for these top 10 venues. It should clearly contrast the win count for the team defending a total against the team chasing a total at that specific stadium.
